Developmental Systems Neuroscience: a Circuit-to-Dynamics approach to study the development of neural systems

My broader scientific interest revolves around understanding how sensory-motor integration gives rise to flexible and adaptive behaviors during early development.

I approach this by combining detailed circuit mapping with population-level analysis of neural activity to reveal the emergent properties of the developing central nervous system.
